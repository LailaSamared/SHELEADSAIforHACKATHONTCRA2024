    About Us

    Our Mission

    To advocate for gender equality in the Tech Industry and empower women to thrive in leadership roles. Through our campaigns and initiatives, we strive to break down barriers, challenge stereotypes, and create a more inclusive and diverse Tech Ecosystem.

    Our Plan

    We are guided by our core values of inclusivity, integrity, and collaboration. We believe in the power of diversity to drive innovation and the importance of creating a supportive and welcoming environment for all individuals in the Tech community.

    Our Vision

    A Tech Industry where every individual, regardless of gender, has equal opportunities to succeed and lead. We envision a future where diversity is celebrated, inclusion is the norm, and women are empowered to reach their full potential in Tech leadership roles

    CAMPAIGN

    Campaigns dedicated to promoting gender diversity and empowering women to take on leadership roles in the Tech Industry.

    #CODE LIKE A GIRL

    The program aims to boost girls' involvement in STEM and coding careers. Participants learn web development basics like HTML, CSS, and Javascript, then create and present websites to judges, peers, and Vodafone representatives.

    #CYBERDADA TZ

    A female-led digital initiative aiming at up-skilling/mentoring and fostering girls with passion and interest in technology and cyber security field.

    #WOMEN AND TECHNOLOGY TANZANIA

    A platform to convene, spotlight women who build, use and promote technology in Tanzania.

    #GIRLS IN ICT HACKATHON TCRA

    Is the competition aims at nurturing innovative ideas and foster leadership in the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) sector,

    #SmartGirlz in ICT Tanzania

    the program provides youth with knowledge, awareness, motivation and network opportunities in the ICT sector. Knowledge gained can enable participating youth to qualify for employment or create self-employment.

    #APPS AND GIRLS

    is a Tanzanian award-winning social enterprise that empowers girls and young women to create the world they want to live in using technology.

    DIGITAL CLUBS

    The presence of digital clubs across Tanzania, through learning institutions from various levels act as campaigns for Tech Industry.

    Visit digitalclubs.tz from TCRA's website to expose yourself to various digital clubs from different levels of education as a student, but also to register and promote yourself as a mentor and own a digital club at your workstation.

    #IPID DIGITAL CLUB

    We enable youths to apply their talents and innovation potentials to pursue their dreams.

    #GENESIS HIGH SCHOOL DIGITAL CLUB

    NON GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATIONS

    The presence of NGO's across Tanzania, act as campaigns for Tech Industry and many other fields of study that empower a female candidate.

    These Organizations promote gender equality in Tanzania, but also provide opportunities for females to pursue and reach their dreams enhancively. building Female leaders.
